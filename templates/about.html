About Ingredible

Ingredible is your go-to app for discovering the best recipes based on the ingredients you have on hand. Whether it's chicken, soy sauce, or salt and pepper, simply enter your ingredients, and Ingredible will find the perfect recipes for you.

How It Works

Ingredible uses a K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) algorithm to search for recipes that match your ingredients. Our dataset is sourced and scraped from Panlasang Pinoy, ensuring authentic and delicious Filipino dishes at your fingertips.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower home cooks by providing easy access to delicious recipes, reducing food waste, and celebrating Filipino cuisine.
